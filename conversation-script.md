# Face-to-Face Conversation Script

## 1. Opening & Greeting

**You:** Namaste bhaiya! Aapka bhel bahut famous hai yahan. Main yahan regular customer hoon.

**You:** Bhaiya, main websites banata hoon mobile phone ke liye. Aapke jaise chhote business owners ke liye. Bas 2 minute sunoge?

---

## 2. Simple Explanation (Avoid Technical Words)

**You:** Dekho bhaiya, aaj kal log kuch bhi dhoondhte hain toh Google pe search karte hain na? "Bhel puri near me" ya "best chaat shop"

**You:** Agar aapki ek chhoti si website hogi mobile pe, toh:
- **Google pe aapka naam aayega** jab koi paas mein bhel dhoondhega
- **Photo dikhengi** aapki special items ki
- **Location map** se log aasani se pahunch jayenge
- **Ek button dabaya** aur seedha aapko call kar sakte hain
- **Timing pata chalegi** ki aap kab khule ho

**You:** Bilkul simple. Jaise aapka ek digital visiting card, jo 24 ghante kaam karta hai.

---

## 3. Building Trust

**You:** Bhaiya, main yahan ke 4-5 shops ke liye already bana chuka hoon. Aapko dikhata hoon mobile pe.

*(Show 1-2 sample websites on your phone)*

**You:** Dekho kitna simple hai. Koi complicated cheez nahi. Bas aapki shop ki achhi photo, timing, aur call button.

---

## 4. Pricing Introduction (Confident & Clear)

**You:** Bhaiya, iske liye main sirf **₹3,000 to ₹5,000** leta hoon.

**Important points:**
- **Ek baar ka payment hai** - bas ek hi baar dena hai
- **Koi monthly charge nahi hai** - na light bill jaisa har mahine kuch dena hai
- **Lifetime rahega** - ek baar bana diya toh hamesha rahega

**You:** Aur dekho, kaam shuru karne ke liye **50% advance** dena hoga. Matlab ₹1,500 to ₹2,500 pehle, baaki ka jab website ready ho jaye tab.

**You:** Yeh isliye taaki main seriously kaam shuru kar sakoon. Aur aapko bhi bharosa rahe ki main pakka karunga.

---

## 5. Closing Question

**You:** Toh bhaiya, kya sochte ho? Kal se shuru kar dete hain?

---

# Objection Handling

## Objection 1: "Yeh toh bahut mehenga hai"

**You:** Bhaiya, samjho aise - agar isse **mahine mein sirf 5 naye customer** bhi aaye, toh 2-3 mahine mein paisa nikal aayega.

**You:** Aur yeh toh **ek baar ka** hai. Pamphlet chhapwate ho na? Woh toh har baar paise lagte hain. Yeh ek baar banaya, hamesha kaam karega.

**You:** Aur dekho, main local hoon. Aap mujhe roz dekh sakte ho. Bhaag thodi na jaunga! *(smile)*

---

## Objection 2: "Mujhe website ki zaroorat nahi hai"

**You:** Bhaiya, main samajh sakta hoon. Aapka business toh already achha chal raha hai.

**You:** Par dekho, aajkal **young generation** sab kuch phone pe dekhti hai. College students, office wale - sab pehle Google karte hain.

**You:** Agar aapki website nahi hai aur paas ki dukaan ki hai, toh woh log wahan chale jayenge. Competition badh raha hai na bhaiya.

**You:** Socho aise - yeh **insurance** jaisa hai. Aapke business ko aage badhne ke liye. Aur sirf ₹3,000-5,000 mein lifetime ke liye.

---

## Objection 3: "Main sochke batata hoon"

**You:** Bilkul bhaiya, soch lo. Koi jaldi nahi hai.

**You:** Par ek baat batau - **Google pe jaldi aana zaroori hai**. Jitna pehle website hogi, utna pehle Google pe dikhoge.

**You:** Aur main is mahine sirf **3-4 websites** hi le raha hoon kyunki achhe se banana hai. Agar delay hua toh phir next month tak wait karna padega.

**You:** Kya main kal aapko ek **sample** banakar dikha sakta hoon? Aapki shop ke naam se. Phir decide kar lena. Free mein dikhaunga, koi charge nahi.

*(This creates urgency + gives them a taste)*

---

# Pro Tips

✅ **Smile and be friendly** - You're helping them, not selling  
✅ **Show samples on your phone** - Visual proof builds trust  
✅ **Use local examples** - "Sharma ji ki shop ke liye banaya tha"  
✅ **Speak their language** - Simple Hindi, no English jargon  
✅ **Be patient** - Don't rush, let them think  
✅ **Follow up** - "Main kal phir aaunga, aap soch lena"

---

**Remember:** You are solving a real problem. Be confident but humble. You're not begging for work - you're offering value.
